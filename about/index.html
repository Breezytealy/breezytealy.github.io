Lottie is a writer and illustrator with science communication firmly at the heart of her works.

She enjoys weaving together a mixture of real and invented science to create immersive works that can both introduce complex scientific theory and extend it into the fantastical. The blurrier the line in her fiction the better.

A trained scientist with an interdisciplinary background, Lottie knows the value of sparking creativity [] and instilling the thirst for knowledge through mediums one would usually not encounter . sparks creativity and encourages people to explore all kinds of science. After all, the truth is often far stranger and more wonderful than fiction!

From fictional textbooks to blogging real research trips, Lottie takes a cross-media approach to sating the curiosity of her readers and followers across all skill levels.
